The skincare industry can be very overwhelming from the variety of products to the dozens of ingredients you should have in them to the dozens of ingredients to avoid. On the opposite end of the spectrum, there is not a lot of range in skincare apps. Skincare apps are either large ecommerce sites or small skincare habit trackers. We wanted to develop an app that is approachable for anyone in their skincare journey, but especially for those new to skincare. With products limited to the basic necessities and quizzes to help users find new products best suited to their skincare needs; an application that helps those new to skincare and a platform for skincare brands to expand their customer base.
